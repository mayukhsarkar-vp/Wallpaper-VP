Our Story

Born from
Obsession

Mayukh Sarkar, a design enthusiast from India, grew tired of settling for mediocre wallpapers — pixelated freebies and generic stock imagery that did nothing for the soul. He spent months searching for wallpapers worthy of modern displays, and found... nothing that matched his vision.

So he built it himself. What started as a personal collection became Velvet Pages — a curated universe of wallpapers that treat your screen like a canvas. Every pack is a mood, a journey, a statement. From whisper-quiet minimalism to intricate, breathtaking detail, Velvet Pages exists at the intersection of art and technology.

Today, Velvet Pages serves customers from Mumbai to Guwahati, Chennai to Chandigarh — people who believe their digital spaces deserve the same care as their physical ones.
